The Art of Florida Outdoor Living

Florida rightfully earned the nickname Sunshine State with its seemingly endless days of sunshine throughout the year. Many residents embrace the outdoor lifestyle, installing pools and patios to enjoy the warm temperatures in the day and sometimes cool evenings. It takes experience and a unique artistry that we offer to design and renovate your outdoor space. Whether you are a long-time resident looking to revamp your outdoor oasis or a new Floridian eager to make the most of your space, lets explore the art of Florida outdoor living through poolside renovations and patio makeovers.

  1. Poolside Elegance The pool is often the focal point of Florida outdoor spaces. Let us upgrade your poolside area with elegant features like mosaic tiles, stylish lighting, and water features. We can create different zones within the pool area ‐ a lounge space for sunbathing, a shaded area for relaxation, and perhaps a tiki bar for entertaining. We use materials that can withstand the Florida sun and are easy to maintain, ensuring your poolside oasis remains inviting year-round.

  2. Al Fresco Dining Florida's climate is perfect for year-round outdoor dining. We can transform your patio into a stylish al fresco dining area. We recommend quality outdoor furniture that complements the design aesthetic and withstands the elements. Consider a pergola or umbrella for shade, and adorn the space with weather-resistant cushions and textiles for a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Adding a flatweave outdoor rug made from recycled plastic or recycled PET fibers for stain and water resistance creates a safe and elegant separation for this space.

  3. Coastal Vibes Some clients look to us to capture the essence of coastal living with a Florida beach-inspired patio makeover. The best color palette for this space is whites, blues, and sandy neutrals. Some coastal-themed design decor to incorporate are seashells, driftwood, and nautical elements. We recommend complementing the design with comfortable outdoor seating that encourages relaxation for the perfect chill, coastal vibes.

  4. Smart Lighting Solutions Florida evenings define paradise. We install lighting that enhances the ambiance of your outdoor space with smart-lighting solutions that easily set and control the mood. String lights and strategically placed lanterns in our designs add to the aesthetics and contribute to the safety and functionality of your outdoor living area.

  5. Fire Features Winter evenings in Florida can sometimes be cool. We can install a fire pit or an outdoor fireplace on your patio - a welcome design element to the outdoor Florida lifestyle. In addition to providing a warm and inviting space, we make it a cozy focal point for outdoor gatherings. Staring into the flickering flames under the starlit Florida sky with friends and family is the perfect way to end a Florida winter day.

Florida's outdoor spaces offer unlimited possibilities for creating your personal piece of paradise. Whether you are envisioning a tranquil poolside retreat, a vibrant patio for entertaining, or a coastal-inspired haven, the art of outdoor living in Florida is about embracing the natural Florida elements, and we can design a space that reflects your outdoor lifestyle with our thoughtful planning process that always stay true to your priorities. We will transform your outdoor area into your personal work of art, inviting you to savor the beauty and warmth of Florida living every day and night.
